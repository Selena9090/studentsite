ABOUT US

Hello Vintage is a modern-day t-shirt line using vintgae graphics. Our brand serves as a convenient alternative to those who admire a retro style but prefer a modern interpretation. We want to let our customers know that this is a place for anyone and everyone that wants to find something that says "this is what i'm feeling right now." Our brand motto is to "Stay Unique!"

Our Mission

We are nuts for vintage style and want to experience a different life through clothing, just like watching your favorite classic 80's film!
