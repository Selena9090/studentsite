Designing Custom Illustrations for Author

Criminal by Phoebe Judge, is a podcast about people who have done wrong, been wronged, or maybe have gotten caught somewhere in the middle. Besides listening to her podcast, what if you were tasked to create trilogy book covers of the stories from Phoebe Judge? After doing a lot of research, listening to multiple podcasts, and endless sketches later, these are the final results of the project.
